About the Builder

A researcher-builder moving from signals to intelligent systems.

Kritim Bastola is originally from Nepal, came to the United States in 2022, earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of New Mexico in 2025, and is now pursuing a Master's in Computer Science at UNM.

Direction

Robotics, intelligent systems, accessibility, and space technology.

Kritim's work connects software with signals from people, devices, physical environments, maps, workflows, and culture. His interests include space, robotics, quantum ideas, AI, accessibility, books, and films.

  • Exploring AI agents, human-centered tools, and speculative intelligence.
